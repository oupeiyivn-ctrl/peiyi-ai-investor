# 📈 Peiyi AI Investor

A personal AI investment assistant designed for long-term investing and options strategies.

## 🎯 Goal

Build an AI assistant that helps make better investment decisions every day.

Instead of reading dozens of news articles and charts, receive one simple daily report.

---

## Daily Report

Every market day:

- Market summary
- QQQ analysis
- Sell Put opportunities
- LEAPS opportunities
- AI stock watchlist
- Important news
- AI recommendation
